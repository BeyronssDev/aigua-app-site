Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Data Collection

Aigua does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app stores your preferences only on your device, including:

  • display name, if you choose to enter one;
  • daily water goal;
  • glass size;
  • reminder schedule;
  • local progress, streaks, and achievements.

This information is stored using Apple local storage on your iPhone and is not sent to any server.

Accounts, Analytics, and Tracking

Aigua does not use accounts, analytics, advertising, third-party SDKs, or tracking.

Notifications

Aigua can schedule optional local notifications to remind you to drink water. Notifications are created on your device using Apple notification APIs. No notification data is sent to a server.

Purchases

Aigua does not include in-app purchases, subscriptions, ads, or paid unlocks.

Health and Medical Disclaimer

Aigua is a personal habit tool. It is not medical advice and does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any condition. If you need health guidance, consult a qualified professional.
