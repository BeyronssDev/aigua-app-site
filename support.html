Common Questions

Where is my data stored?

Progress, settings, reminders, streaks, and achievements are stored locally on your iPhone.

Does Aigua collect analytics?

No. Aigua does not use analytics, ads, tracking, accounts, or third-party SDKs.

Is Aigua medical advice?

No. Aigua is a personal habit tool and is not medical advice.
